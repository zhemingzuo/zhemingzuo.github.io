About Me

Zheming Zuo is a Research Fellow in the School of Computer Science, at the University of Birmingham, U.K., working with Dr Shuo Wang on the EPSRC project of "Adaptive Multi-Source Transfer Learning Approaches for Environmental Challenges". Prior to that, he was a postdoctoral research associate (PDRA) at Newcastle University and University of Durham, working with Prof Boguslaw Obara in Scalable Computing Group on deep learning for commercial brand protection, and Dr Noura Al Moubayed in Vision, Imaging and Visualisation in Durham (ViVID) on machine learning for pervasive healthcare. His research interests include Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Fuzzy Logic, and their industrial applications.
